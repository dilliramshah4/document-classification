Fellowships and Externally Funded Grants:

NIH Predoctoral Traineeship (4 years) 1969-1973.

NSF Research Project Grant (3 years) 1979-1981. T was sole Pf for this grant, to
investigate mechanisms of speciation.

National Research Service Award for Senior Fellows from NIH (1 year) 1982-1983. This
funded a year’s leave of absence, to work with Dr. Jeff Hall at Brandeis U.

Scheinfeld Visiting Professorship from The Scheinfeld Center for the Study of Human
Genetics in the Social Sciences, Hebrew U. (1 year) 1989. This funds a year's
sabbatical leave, to work with Drs. Ruth Guttman (Hebrew U., Jerusalem) and Dan Segal
(Tel Aviv U., Tel Aviv).
